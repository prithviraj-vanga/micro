How to Use the Let's Speak Up

For Users (Submitting a Complaint)

  1. Open the Website

  2. Visit the homepage of the Complaint Management System.

  3. Go to Complaint Form

  4. Click on the “Register Complaint” option

  5. Fill in Details

  6. Enter required information

  7. Click on the “Submit” button to register your complaint

  8. Get Complaint ID

After submission, a unique Complaint ID will be generated. Save it for future tracking.

Track Complaint Status

Go to the “Track Complaint” section
and enter your Complaint ID to check status
(Pending / Resolved).



Quick Tip

  • Always keep your Complaint ID safe to track
    your issue easily.

  • Ensure correct details are entered to avoid delays in resolution.
